About Us

Bonsai Gardening was born in April of 2020 during the global CoronaVirus Pandemic. As cites across the United States were shut down to help "flatten the curve" this left individuals and families locked in their homes with no way to experience the beautiful spring bloom. We decided to start Bonsai Gardens to share the beauty of our outdoor gardens and to help spread positivity to those who were unable to leave their homes. Here you can virtually explore our gardens, and find beautiful flowers as they bloom daily! We hope we've helped you find a little beauty, positivity and joy in your day.
